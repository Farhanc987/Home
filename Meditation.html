What can meditation do for your mental health

Meditation can produce a deep state of relaxation and a tranquil mind. During meditation, you focus your attention and eliminate the stream of jumbled thoughts that may be crowding your mind and causing stress. This process may result in enhanced physical and emotional well-being. The health benefits of meditation are endless as it not only helps your mental state it can also improve your physical state, meditation had shown to reduce depression relapses by 12%, increasing happiness and also improving memory in some cases.
